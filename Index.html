⚡ HOW TO PLAY ⚡
◆ CONTROLS
🕹️
Move — Joystick (touch) or Mouse
⚡
Boost SPACE HOLD — speed burst, drains your length
🔥
Power Q — unique species ability, 9s cooldown
⛶
Full Screen — tap ⛶ on any screen
⏸
Pause — top-left button or P / ESC
◆ DAMAGE RULES — HEAD ONLY
💥
Your head hits enemy body → YOU take their ATK damage
💢
Two heads collide → BOTH snakes take damage
🧱
Hit the wall → lose a life. No body-on-body damage ever.
🎯
Abilities & projectiles always hit the head directly
◆ LIVES & SCORING
🌟
Start with 3 lives, earn up to 5 max
❤️
Free life at 10k pts — threshold doubles: 20k → 40k → 80k → 160k
💀
On death: respawn at half length, half your score drops as collectible orbs
🏆
Kill a snake → steal 200 pts + bonus orbs from their body
📈
View auto-zooms out as your snake grows — the bigger you get, the more you see
◆ DIFFICULTY
🟢
Easy — slow bots, low aggression, 8 opponents
🟡
Medium — standard challenge, 12 opponents
🟠
Hard — fast AI reactions, high aggression, 14 opponents
🔴
Insane — near-instant AI, maximum speed & coordination, 14 opponents
◆ SNAKE CLASSES
Glass Cannon — 1 HP, lightning fast, devastating powers. High risk, high reward.
Balanced — medium stats across the board. Forgiving and versatile.
Tank — slow but nearly unkillable. High HP, powerful defensive abilities.
◆ FIGHTER TIPS
🎨
Drag the color wheel on fighter select to customize your snake's skin pattern
🔒
Use the 🔒 button in-game to lock your zoom depth — saved across sessions
⚡
Each snake has a passive ability active at all times + a power on 9s cooldown
🐍
Bots use species-specific AI — Anaconda orbits you, King Cobra zones, Mamba rushes
